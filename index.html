ABOUT ME

A graduate from the University of Łódź, Faculty of Law and from the Warsaw School of Economics, Finance and Accounting.

For many years associated with the financial sector. I worked at Polish Financial Supervision Authority, Warsaw Stock Exchange and Alior Bank Group, holding positions: senior clerk, senior specialist, acting Risk Officer, Operational Risk Manager and Business Continuity Coordinator.

While working on the financial market, I was involved in building an operational risk management system, including the creation of risk management tools and the development of organizational culture in the area of risk. Also involved in the development of the business continuity management system.

I participated in creating business assumptions for the module supporting risk management in the Governace Risk Compliance system.
